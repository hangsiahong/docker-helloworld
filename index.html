Power of AI

From Prompt to Powerful Mini-App

Opal combines the capabilities of Google's leading AI models (like Gemini and Imagen) into sophisticated, multi-step workflows. We call these workflows **Opals**.

  • ⇒

    Code-Free Creation

    Use natural language to describe your app idea, and Opal builds the initial workflow automatically.

  • ⇒

    Instant Publishing

    Share your custom AI tools instantly via a unique Google link. No hosting, no setup.

  • ⇒

    Full Visual Control

    Fine-tune the logic with our node-based editor. Change prompts, add tools, and manage data flow visually.
